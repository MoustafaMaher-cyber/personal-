The Rehabilitation Methodology

A structured, science-backed approach to neuroplasticity and recovery.

1

Comprehensive Assessment

In-depth clinical evaluation of motor, sensory, and cognitive functions to establish a baseline.

2

Goal Setting

Establishment of meaningful, functional goals based on evidence-based protocols and clinical data.

3

Targeted Intervention

Implementation of intensive, repetitive, and task-specific therapies to drive neuroplasticity.

4

Neuromodulation Technologies

Integrating advanced modalities such as TMS, TES, TPS, LASER, and Trans-Spinal Stimulation to enhance neuroplasticity and recovery.

5

Continuous Re-evaluation

Regular monitoring of progress and dynamic adjustment of the treatment plan for optimal outcomes.
